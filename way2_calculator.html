How it works

1
You invest in USD (converted from UZS at Central Bank rate on the day)
2
Akfa Aluminium pays 13.6% per year — split into 2 payments every 6 months
3
You receive dividends in UZS (converted back at the rate on payment day)
4
You choose: withdraw to your card or reinvest for compounding returns

Dividend formula

D = I × (13.6% ÷ 365) × P

D— dividend you receive ($)
I— your investment amount ($)
P— number of days invested

Use 366 instead of 365 for leap years (e.g. 2024).

Example

Investment$5,500
Start date1 July 2024
Days invested184 days (Jul 1 → Dec 31)
Calculation5,500 × (13.6% ÷ 366) × 184
Dividend earned$376
